## 🎓 MODEL LITERACY

**Effective Context Length vs. Advertised Context Window**: DeepSeek-V4 claims a million tokens. GPT-5.5 advertises 256K. Claude offers 200K standard, 1M in beta. But the number on the box isn't the number that matters — effective context is how much of that input the model actually attends to and reasons over. Research consistently shows models degrade when relevant information is buried deep in long contexts (the "lost in the middle" problem). For builders choosing between V4, GPT-5.5, and Claude for agentic workloads, test retrieval accuracy at your actual input lengths — a model with 200K effective context beats one with 1M advertised but 100K effective every time.
