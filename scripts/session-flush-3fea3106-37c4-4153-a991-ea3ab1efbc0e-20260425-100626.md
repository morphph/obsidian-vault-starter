## 🎓 MODEL LITERACY

**Mixture of Experts (MoE) and Inference Cost**: DeepSeek V4 matches frontier performance at 1/20th the price — and the secret is Mixture of Experts architecture. A traditional "dense" model activates every parameter for every token, so a 1-trillion-parameter model does 1 trillion calculations per token. MoE models split their parameters into specialized "expert" sub-networks and only route each token to a small handful of them — say 8 out of 256 experts. The result: a model with a trillion total parameters might only use 30-50 billion per token, slashing compute cost while retaining the knowledge capacity of the full network. This is why cost-performance disruptions keep coming from architecture innovation, not just raw scale — and why the "most expensive model wins" assumption is increasingly wrong.
