## 🎓 MODEL LITERACY

**Scaling Laws vs. Data Efficiency**: Scaling laws predict that bigger models trained on more data perform better — and they've held remarkably well for years. But today's results challenge the orthodoxy from both ends: DeepSeek V4 Flash shows that optimizing for speed and efficiency at a given scale can beat larger, slower models on practical workloads, while the 250-example SWE-bench result proves that surgical fine-tuning on high-quality data can unlock capabilities you'd expect only from frontier-scale models. The takeaway: scaling laws describe averages, not ceilings. When you know exactly what task you're solving, a small model with curated data can outperform a general-purpose giant — and understanding where that crossover happens tells you when to reach for a frontier API and when to fine-tune your own.
