## 🎓 MODEL LITERACY

**Context Engineering**: Today's agent view launch and agentic search coverage both tackle the same core problem: routing the right information into the right context window at the right time. Context engineering — structuring, selecting, and compressing information for LLM consumption — is why multi-session agents need dashboards and why most agent failures are retrieval failures, not reasoning failures. A model with perfect reasoning but the wrong context will confidently produce the wrong answer. When you hear "context window," think less about the size limit and more about what goes into it — that curation step is where most of the real engineering happens.
