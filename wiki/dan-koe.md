# Dan Koe

## Summary
Content creator (@thedankoe) focused on **personal brand, online business, writing, and self-development**. Major X long-form writer with mass audience reach. First **content-creator / self-development** voice in our wiki — distinct from the engineering / VC perspectives dominant elsewhere. His 2026-01-27 piece "How to think like a strategic genius (5D thinking)" hit **2.17M views and 22,381 bookmarks** — the highest bookmark-to-like ratio (2.14×) among recent ingests, signaling readers treat his work as durable reference.

## Details

### Single longform in wiki (so far)
**2026-01-27 — How to think like a strategic genius (5D thinking)** ([[source-dankoe-strategic-genius-thinking]]). Pedagogically condenses Ken Wilber's Integral Theory (AQAL framework + developmental psychology) into a builder/creator-friendly model:
- **Lines** of thinking = horizontal width (domain knowledge)
- **Levels** of thinking = vertical depth (cognitive development, 5 stages)
- **Altitude** of thinking = cross-dimensional height (the *average* of all your levels)
- **4 quadrants** = inner/outer × individual/collective
- **5D** = adding TIME (history / evolutionary patterns)

See [[five-levels-of-thinking]] for the standalone concept.

### Why his voice matters in this wiki
- **Highest bookmark/like ratio of any recent ingest** (2.14×) — readers treat his work as durable reference, not entertainment
- **Mass-market reach** (2.17M views on a single article) — useful temperature read on what creator-economy / personal-brand audiences are absorbing
- **Bridges philosophy and practice** — packages Ken Wilber-style developmental frameworks for action-oriented audiences (founders, writers, online builders), which is the user profile our wiki primarily serves
- **First non-engineering perspective**: balances the AI-engineering and VC-thesis heavy coverage with first-principles thinking content

### Position relative to the wiki's domain
The CLAUDE.md domain focus includes "People and their ideas" + content distribution. Dan Koe is a **canonical example of a content creator who turns frameworks into shareable longform** — useful both as a thinker (his frameworks) and as a case study (his packaging).

### Bias / limitations
- Dan Koe's audience is primarily the **personal-brand / online-business** segment — frameworks are pitched at action-takers, not academic readers
- The "Levels" framework is **Ken Wilber's Integral Theory, repackaged.** Dan Koe makes it accessible but doesn't cite Wilber explicitly (he mentions Wilber once in passing). Useful to know if you want to dig deeper
- "5D" framing is rhetorical (it's the 4D model + time, not really 5 dimensions)
