# Source: A Brief History of Ralph

## Summary
Dex Horthy's (HumanLayer) complete timeline of the Ralph Wiggum technique from June 2025 to January 2026. Documents the "overbaking phenomenon," community evolution, bash-loop vs plugin debate, and key milestones.

## Source Details
- **URL**: https://www.humanlayer.dev/blog/brief-history-of-ralph
- **Author**: Dex Horthy (HumanLayer)
- **Type**: Historical blog post

## Key Claims
1. June 2025: first encountered at Twitter community meetup
2. July 2025: Huntley's official launch (originally using Amp/Sourcegraph)
3. August 2025: shipped 6 repositories overnight; regenerating > rebasing
4. September 2025: CURSED lang launched (compiled to Zig)
5. December 2025: Anthropic official plugin released; "dies in cryptic ways" without --dangerously-skip-permissions
6. Bash loop (fresh context) vs plugin (same session) is fundamental architectural debate
7. Horthy still prefers "5-line bash loops" over official plugin
