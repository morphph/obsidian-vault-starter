# Source: Inventing the Ralph Wiggum Loop — Geoffrey Huntley Interview

## Summary
Dev Interrupted / LinearB newsletter + 58-minute podcast with Geoffrey Huntley (Jan 14, 2026). Only the newsletter text was captured — the podcast audio was not transcribed. Newsletter confirms key claims: "the loop is the hero, not the model," Gas Town concept, MEOW (Molecular Expression of Work).

## Key Claims (from newsletter text only)
1. "The loop is the hero, not the model" — paradigm shift from model intelligence to system persistence
2. Gas Town = "Kubernetes for agents" (Steve Yegge concept) — orchestration of scaling numbers of AI agents
3. MEOW (Molecular Expression of Work) = granular task definitions for parallel agent coordination
4. "Software development as a profession is effectively dead; software engineering is more alive than ever"
5. Topics covered in audio: context rot, compaction avoidance, building autonomous agent systems

## Limitations
The 58-minute podcast likely contains significantly more detail on Huntley's philosophy, CURSED lang war stories, Gas Town architecture, and practical advice. Only the newsletter summary blurbs were captured.
