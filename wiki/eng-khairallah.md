# Khairallah AL-Awady

## Summary
Self-described "angel investor | founder @Web3Arabs | vibe coding | ai & onchain research" (X bio). Author of the 2.4M-view May 2026 longform [[3-agent-starter-team|"How to Build a Team of AI Agents That Replace Your First 3 Hires"]] — a packaged playbook aimed at solo founders rather than AI-tooling builders. Less architecturally novel than [[garry-tan|Garry Tan]]'s Resolver/Skills work, but **far higher mass-market reach** (2.4M views vs Garry's 293K on the Resolvers piece) — useful as a temperature read on what mainstream founders are now hearing about agent-building.

## Details

- **X handle:** @eng_khairallah1, verified account
- **Affiliated:** Web3Arabs (founder)
- **Topic mix:** vibe coding, AI workflows, on-chain research, automation playbooks
- **Audience:** solo founders / early-stage operators in the "I have revenue but can't afford 3 hires" stage
- **Style:** packaged playbooks with "what to do" checklists at the end of each section. Heavy on framing ("the smartest solo founders are not hiring, they are building"), explicit about the 70-80% honest coverage claim

### Public influence (one data point)
- 2.4M views, 7.5K bookmarks, 2,197 likes on a single longform article (May 5, 2026)
- The bookmark-to-like ratio (~3.4×) is unusually high — readers are saving this as a reference, not just liking. Stronger signal of practical-utility intent than typical viral AI content.

### Contributions to our wiki

- Articulated the [[prompt-architecture-three-layer|three-layer prompt architecture]] (System / Workflow / Output) as a builder-friendly mental model
